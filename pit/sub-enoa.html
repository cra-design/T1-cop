NOA in tax software

You may be looking for:

  • NOA in tax software for professional tax preparers

You can view the notice of assessment (NOA) or notice of reassessment (NOR) in tax software right after the return has been processed by the CRA.

The amounts on the NOA in tax software is the same information as the notice the CRA will send you.

Refer to: Notices of assessment

Ensure you can access your CRA account

You must be registered and have access to your CRA account.

If you are having problems signing in, refer to: Help with using your CRA account

Filing a tax return for someone else

If you are filing a tax return for someone else, you will have to register as a representative.

You will also have to request authorization to access someone else's tax information.

Tax software for filing personal taxes

You may be looking for:

  • Filing as a professional tax preparer

You can use certified tax software to help calculate and file your personal income taxes electronically. The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) certifies the tax software provided by third party software developers for each tax year.

The CRA provides a list of certified tax software products to help you find an option that meets the needs of your tax situation. Within certified tax software, you can choose how you want to fill in your tax information, send your return to the CRA, and view your notice of assessment (NOA).

Sending the tax return

After completing your return in certified tax software, you have the option to electronically send the return to the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA). Once you receive your confirmation, you may be able to view your notice in tax software.

On this page:

  • Send the return electronically
  • Confirm the return was received
  • After using tax software

Send the return electronically

You can use the "NETFILE" service in tax software to electronically send the tax return to the CRA.

The service is available daily, except between:

  • 3 am and 6 am ET for maintenance
  • February 1 to 24, 2025

Most Canadian residents can use the "NETFILE" service. If you are unsure about any restrictions that may apply to your situation, you can check to confirm you can use this service.

If you cannot use the "NETFILE" service, you can print your return and mail it to the CRA.

CRA security of your personal information

We accept responsibility for the security of information once we receive it. We take precautions to ensure that there is no unauthorized access to your data, and ensure the confidentiality of data you send using NETFILE. We use sophisticated security and encryption to protect this website and your personal information.

We are also responsible for making sure personal and financial information is sent in an encrypted format between your computer and our servers. This ensures that computer hackers and other Internet users can’t view or alter the data you send to us.

Confirm the return was received

You will get a confirmation message in your tax software after sending the return to the CRA.

The message will include a code, which confirms the CRA has successfully received your completed tax return.

Viewing your notice in tax software

In tax software, you can view the notice of assessment (NOA) right after sending the return.

If you select this option, you will be prompted to enter your CRA account user ID and password after receiving your confirmation message.

For details: NOA in tax software

After using tax software

There are 3 possible outcomes from your tax return:

  • Get a refund
  • Have a balance owing
  • Have no refund or balance owing

You will receive your notice of assessment (NOA) after your tax return is processed. It will indicate the outcome from your tax return.

Processing time: Within 2 weeks

For details, refer to: After filing a tax return
